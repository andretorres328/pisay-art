Pisay Art

Welcome to the Pisay Art (Also known as P. Art) website! We are a pair of students in Philippine Science Highschool that are calligraphy enthusiasts. We created this website to document and showcase how our calligraphy style both improves and develops. We are trying to prove that Pisay scholars are not just academically-talented, but we can also be artistically-talented if we try hard enough.

We hope you enjoy what you will see and, if you liked it that much, you would also want us to do some calligraphy for you. :)
