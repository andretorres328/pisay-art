What is a nib pen?

A nib pen is a writing material that uses an external ink to write. Nowadays, the nib pen is used for calligraphy because it exhibits a different variety of thickness thanks to its flexible nib. It can also be used for shading in manga and comics.

How does it work?

Since the nib has a space allocated for the storage of the ink, it must be properly handled to have the best result. First, the user must dip the nib into the calligraphy ink (any other ink won't work properly. Then, the user has to create an angle between the pen and paper that needs to create an angle to the paper. Remember that the traditional rules must be followed. In downstrokes, the user must exert force and pressure to the pen to give a good amount of ink that shows thick lines and for upstrokes, light pressure is only needed.
